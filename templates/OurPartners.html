Our Partners

PT PLN ( PERSERO ) Electricity For A Better Life

PT Binsar Natorang Energi has become a good partner with PT Perusahaan Listrik Negara (PLN) to distribute electricity to the people.In accordance with the Government Regulation Number 18,the status of PT Perusahaan Listrik Nasional (PLN) is the Holder of the Electricity Business Authority (PKUK), which is in charge of providing electricity for the public interest.

Short Story PLN

PLN, which stands for Perusahaan Listrik Negara, is the state-owned electricity company in Indonesia. Established on October 27, 1945, PLN is responsible for generating, transmitting, and distributing electricity across the archipelago. With a mission to provide reliable and affordable electricity to the people of Indonesia, PLN plays a pivotal role in supporting economic growth, industrial development, and improving the quality of life for millions of Indonesians. The company has also been actively involved in promoting renewable energy sources to reduce environmental impact and ensure a sustainable energy future for the nation.
