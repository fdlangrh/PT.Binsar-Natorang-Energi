About Us


Know Us Better


The construction of the Hasang hydropower plant, PT. Binsar Natorang Energi follows the applicable laws and regulations, namely Law no. 32 of 2009 concerning Environmental Protection and Management, in terms of providing information related to environmental protection and management in a correct, accurate, open and timely manner,maintaining the sustainability of environmental functions and complying with provisions on environmental quality standards.

PT Binsar Natorang Energi is a limited entity engaged in the electric power industry through hydro power plants. PT Binsar Natorang Energi was established on April 25, 2008 in accordance with the Decree of the Minister of Law and Human Rights Number: AHU-21249.AH.01.01.Tahun 2008. PT. Binsar Natorang Energi has developed a Hydro electric Power Plant in North Sumatra, Indonesia, supported by LG International and PT. Titan Multi Power. By utilizing the Water Resources of the Kualu River, PT Binsar Natorang Energi participates in the Water Resources Development and Conservation Program to increase the utilization of the Water Resources function for the provision of national electrical energy.
